## Topics of the course

-  Euler Tours
- Hamilton Cycles
- Trees
- Spaces of Graphs
- Planar Graphs
- Networks and Connectivity
- Matching and Independence Number
- Colouring
- Graphs and Groups
- Turan Theorem
- Ramsey Theorem
- Point-Set Theory
- Hypergraphs

Basic linear algebra, calculus.
